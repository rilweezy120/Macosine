Our Services

With a team of top-tier engineers, scientists, and investment professionals,
we are committed to delivering innovative solutions that push the boundaries of what is possible.

Energy Product Development and Manufacturing

We develop and manufacture cutting-edge energy products that meet the needs of utility, commercial, and industrial sectors. Our team of experts uses innovative technology and engineering to create products that are durable, reliable, and energy-efficient.

Power Equipment Distribution

Our power equipment distribution services provide our clients with access to top-notch power equipment and solutions that cater to their energy needs. We distribute various types of equipment, including prefabricated substations, smart electric meters, HV switchgear, low voltage switchgear, and more.

Energy Management System

Our energy management systems help our clients monitor, control, and optimize their energy usage to improve efficiency and reduce costs. We provide customized solutions that cater to our clients' specific energy needs, using innovative technology to create scalable systems that can adapt to the changing demands of their businesses.

Data and Cloud Services

At Macosine, we understand the importance of data management in ensuring a smooth and efficient operation of our client's businesses. That's why we offer data and cloud services to help our clients capture, store, manage, and analyze their data. Our cloud services also provide a secure and convenient way for clients to access their data from anywhere, at any time.

Oil and Gas Service Contracting

We offer oil and gas service contracting to our clients, providing them with high-quality solutions that ensure maximum productivity and efficiency. Our services are tailored to the specific needs of our clients, with a focus on safety and compliance to international standards.
